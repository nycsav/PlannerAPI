## Target Audience Clarity

### Who This Is For:
✅ **Brand Marketers** - CMOs, VP Marketing, Brand Directors
✅ **Advertising Strategists** - Media planners, account strategists, creative strategists
✅ **Agency Professionals** - Agency owners, strategy leads, new business teams
✅ **Ad Tech Professionals** - Platform strategists, programmatic buyers

### What They Need:
- Real-time industry news (not 24-hour-old articles)
- Competitive intelligence (what agencies/brands are doing)
- Platform updates (TikTok, Meta, Google changes)
- Trend analysis (retail media, AI ads, CTV shifts)
- Strategic briefs (not just news reporting)

### Why They Choose This Over Ad Age:
1. **Speed** - Real-time search vs published articles
2. **Specificity** - Ask exact questions vs browse categories
3. **Format** - Structured briefs vs long-form articles
4. **AI Analysis** - Signals + implications vs raw reporting
5. **Searchability** - Query anything vs limited archives
